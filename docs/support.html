Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore purchases?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will verify your App Store receipt and unlock any previously purchased non-consumable items.

My gems didn't show up!

This is rare but can happen if the network drops. Email us with your Username and a screenshot of the receipt, and we will credit your account manually within 24 hours.

Is the game free?

Yes! Nertz Royale is free to play. All card packs and cosmetics are optional.
