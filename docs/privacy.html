Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Nertz Royale respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide the game service:

  • Account information (username, email if provided)
  • Game statistics (wins, rank, matches played)
  • Device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting

3. How We Use Data

We use your data to:

  • Match you with opponents of similar skill
  • Track your progress on leaderboards
  • Improve game stability and performance

4. Data Deletion

You may request account deletion at any time by contacting support@nertz.best or using the "Delete Account" option in the app settings.

5. Third Party Services

We use Supabase for backend services and Unity Ads (if applicable) for rewards.
