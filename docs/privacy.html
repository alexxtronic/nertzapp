Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Nertz Royale ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide the game service:

  • Account Information: Username and user ID generated upon sign-up. Email address is only stored if you sign in with Apple/Google authentication.
  • Game Statistics: Matches played, wins/losses, rank points, and mission progress.
  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous device IDs used for analytics, crash reporting (via Firebase), and preventing duplicate accounts.

3. How We Use Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Match you with opponents of similar skill level.
  • Track your progress on global leaderboards.
  • Process in-app purchases and restore inventory.
  • Improve game stability and fix crashes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your game data is stored securely on Supabase servers (hosted on AWS/Google Cloud). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Data Deletion

Relationships are temporary. You may request full account deletion at any time by:

  • Using the "Delete Account" button in the app Settings.
  • Emailing us at alexdamore2@gmail.com with your request.

Upon deletion, all your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For backend database, authentication, and realtime features.
  • RevenueCat / Apple StoreKit: For processing payments. We do not store credit card details.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at alexdamore2@gmail.com.
